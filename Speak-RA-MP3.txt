Your thesis #41624

Original:
It is normally expected that the final version of your thesis which must be submitted to the university library in both hardcopy and electronic form will be freely available to the public. Once in the library, your thesis may be consulted, borrowed and copied in accordance with the regulations.

Chinese translation:
人们通常预期，你论文的最终版本必须以纸质版和电子版两种形式提交给大学图书馆，并将免费向公众开放。论文进入图书馆后，可以按照相关规定被查阅、借阅和复制。

Highlighted words:
1. thesis - 论文
2. electronic - 电子的
3. consulted - 被查阅；被咨询
4. accordance - 依照；按照
5. regulations - 规定；规章制度

Psychology #41578

Original:
Psychology is the study of cognitions, emotions, and behavior. Psychologists are involved in a variety of tasks. Many spend their careers designing and performing research to understand how people behave in specific situations, how and why we think the way we do, and how emotions develop and what impact they have on our interactions with others.

Chinese translation:
心理学是对认知、情绪和行为的研究。心理学家参与各种各样的任务。许多人将自己的职业生涯用于设计和开展研究，以了解人们在特定情境中如何表现，我们如何以及为什么会以某种方式思考，以及情绪如何发展并对我们与他人的互动产生什么影响。

Highlighted words:
1. impact - 影响；作用
2. interactions - 互动；相互交流

Changes in climate #41566

Original:
Changes in climate affect, for example, the plant and animal life of a given area. The presence of coal beds in North America and Europe along with evidence of glaciation in these same areas indicates that they must have experienced alternately warmer and colder climates than they now possess.

Chinese translation:
气候变化会影响某一特定地区的动植物生存状况。北美和欧洲存在煤层，再加上这些相同地区存在冰川作用的证据，表明这些地区一定曾经历过比现在更温暖和更寒冷的交替气候。

Highlighted words:
1. presence - 存在；出现
2. coal - 煤
3. glaciation - 冰川作用；冰川化
4. indicates - 表明；显示
5. alternately - 交替地；轮流地
6. possess - 拥有；具有

Application of information technology

Original:
In medicine, the application of information technology means the digitalization of medical records and the establishment of an intelligent network for sharing those records. Other benefits flow from these technological changes. In the past medicine has taken a paternalistic stance, with the all-knowing physician dispensing wisdom, but that is becoming increasingly untenable.

Chinese translation:
在医学领域，信息技术的应用意味着医疗记录的数字化，以及建立一个用于共享这些记录的智能网络。其他益处也随这些技术变化而来。过去，医学采取的是一种家长式的立场，由无所不知的医生传授智慧，但这种做法正变得越来越难以维持。

Highlighted words:
1. digitalization - 数字化
2. records - 记录；档案
3. paternalistic - 家长式的；专断保护式的
4. stance - 立场；态度
5. physician - 医生；内科医生
6. dispensing - 分发；提供；给予
7. untenable - 站不住脚的；难以维持的
