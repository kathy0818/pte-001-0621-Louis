Your thesis #41624

Original:
It is normally expected that the final version of your thesis which must be submitted to the university library in both hardcopy and electronic form will be freely available to the public. Once in the library, your thesis may be consulted, borrowed and copied in accordance with the regulations.

Chinese translation:
人们通常预期，你论文的最终版本必须以纸质版和电子版两种形式提交给大学图书馆，并将免费向公众开放。论文进入图书馆后，可以按照相关规定被查阅、借阅和复制。

Highlighted words:
1. thesis - 论文
2. electronic - 电子的
3. consulted - 被查阅；被咨询
4. accordance - 依照；按照
5. regulations - 规定；规章制度

Psychology #41578

Original:
Psychology is the study of cognitions, emotions, and behavior. Psychologists are involved in a variety of tasks. Many spend their careers designing and performing research to understand how people behave in specific situations, how and why we think the way we do, and how emotions develop and what impact they have on our interactions with others.

Chinese translation:
心理学是对认知、情绪和行为的研究。心理学家参与各种各样的任务。许多人将自己的职业生涯用于设计和开展研究，以了解人们在特定情境中如何表现，我们如何以及为什么会以某种方式思考，以及情绪如何发展并对我们与他人的互动产生什么影响。

Highlighted words:
1. impact - 影响；作用
2. interactions - 互动；相互交流
